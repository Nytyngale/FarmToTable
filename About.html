Our mission


Farm to Table is a delivery and pick up service that sells locally grown produce to families in the Bay Area. We pride ourselves in our commitment with the community and local farmers and we focus on delivering our produce in a timely manner and at affordable prices. The covid 19 pandemic has hit farmers tremendously and we want to help them sell their products. We try to minimize contact to keep everyone safe, clients can choose to pick up their groceries at a designated location and we will walk the bags to their car and place them in their trunck We operate from different locations in the Bay Area.
